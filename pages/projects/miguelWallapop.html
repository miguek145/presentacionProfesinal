Shop of shops

This application is about an online shop that sells products from several shops, the users of each company can add their products in the app and then sell them, also the app has LOGIN/REGISTRATION and a product search engine that I have done with AJAX and jquery, on the other hand I have used PHP sessions and cookies and the data is stored in a database.
