UNESCO World Heritage Sites in Spain

What is a WH Site?

It is a landmark or area with legal protection by an international convention administered by the UNESCO. World Heritage Sites are designated by UNESCO for having cultural, historical, scientific or other form of significance.

How many WH Sites are there in Spain?

Spain has 49 total sites inscribed on the list, only behind China (55), Italy (55) and Germany (51). Of these 49 sites, 43 are cultural, 4 are natural, and 2 are mixed (meeting both cultural and natural criteria), as determined by the organization's selection criteria.
